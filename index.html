Deutsche Telekom for MVNOs

Deutsche Telekom is one of the world's leading integrated telecommunications companies. We operate mobile networks in 15 European countries as well as the US and offer the only cross-Atlantic mobile telecommunications footprint in the world.

Our goal is to innovate our industry and provide our customers with the best possible service. We believe that partnerships are essential to achieving this objective and enabling Mobile Virtual Network Operators (MVNOs) is one the most important partnership forms in our strategy to achieve this objective.

We are one of the pioneers in the Virtual Operator field and we host a number of the most successful MVNOs of the world on our networks. Our extensive experience and knowledge means that we can provide our partners with the best possible support to drive successful entry in the communications space.

Selected Segments

Offering mobile services allows our partners to monetize existing assets and make existing products and services more compelling. If you are active in one of the following industries, there is significant potential in partnering with us to offer mobile telecommunications services. There is also potential beyond these industries, and no limit to the innovation potential of MVNO services!

Retailers

  • Increase footfall in own points of sales
  • Improve loyalty of customer base with cross segment/category campaigns
  • Exploit revenues from mobile services and devices

Ethnic Brands

  • Monetize international calling routes and customer base
  • Provide best value mobile service for international calls
  • Target ethnic groups with individual mobile services

Youth Brands

  • Monetize brand awareness and media reach in teenagers and young adults segment
  • Grow customers' brand interaction time and content consumption
  • Stimulate brand loyalty

Social Networks

  • Provide specific price propositions for the mobile social network usage
  • Monetize user base by adding social commerce and service extras
  • Encourage user stickiness by integrating mobile services into core of brand proposition

Fixed Operators

  • Provide full telecommunications portfolio to subscriber base
  • Reduce churn by offering multi-play bundles
  • Leverage existing network assets to provide integrated services

Data

  • Improve product appeal by bundling devices and connectivity
  • Offer advanced services (e.g. M2M, maintenance service)
  • Add continuous service revenues to one-time device sales revenues

MVNO Business Models

We are experienced in large a variety of business models, complementing our partners' assets, competencies and investments. Jointly with our partners, we select the best fitting commercial and technical set-up for each co-operation.

Our solution portfolio ranges from brand licensing/branded reselling to national roaming - and includes any model in between. In the first case we operationally manage the whole mobile service delivery and rely on our partners' branding and marketing assets, in the latter case our partner runs an own mobile (core) network.

Branded Resale/ Brand Licensing

For partners for whom telco is suitable brand extension, but not a core business.

Our main contribution

  • Operations and business support

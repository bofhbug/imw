Deutsche Telekom for MVNOs

Deutsche Telekom is one of the world's leading integrated telecommunications companies. We operate mobile networks in 15 European countries as well as the US and offer the only cross-Atlantic mobile telecommunications footprint in the world.

Our goal is to innovate our industry and provide our customers with the best possible service. Essential to achieving this objective are strong partnerships, of which one of the most important is the enablement of Mobile Virtual Network Operators (MVNOs).

We are a pioneer in the Virtual Operator field and host a number of the world's most successful MVNOs on our networks. With our extensive knowledge, we give our partners the support they need to successfully enter the telecommunications space.

Selected Segments

By offering mobile services, our partner's products and services become more compelling and existing assets can be monetized. If you are active in one of the following industries, there is significant potential in partnering with us to offer mobile telecommunications services. Beyond these fields there are limitless innovation possibilities with MVNO services!

Retailers

  • Increase points of sales footfall
  • Improve loyalty of customer base with cross segment/category campaigns
  • Exploit revenues from mobile services and devices

Ethnic Brands

  • Monetize international calling routes and customer base
  • Provide best value mobile service for international calls
  • Target ethnic groups with individual mobile services

Youth Brands

  • Monetize brand awareness and media reach with teenager and young adult segments
  • Grow customers' brand interaction time and content consumption
  • Stimulate brand loyalty

Social Networks

  • Provide specific propositions for mobile social network use
  • Monetize user base by adding social commerce and service extras
  • Encourage user loyalty by integrating mobile services into core brand proposition

Fixed Operators

  • Provide full telecommunications portfolio to subscriber base

Data

  • Improve product appeal by bundling devices and connectivity

MVNO Business Models

We have experience with a large variety of business models that complement our partners' assets, competencies and investments. Together with our partners, we select the most appropriate commercial and technical set-up for each project.

Our solution portfolio ranges from brand licensing/branded reselling to national roaming - and includes any model in-between. In the first case, we operationally manage the whole mobile service delivery and rely on our partners' branding and marketing assets. In the latter case, our partners run their own mobile (core) network.

Branded Resale/
Brand Licensing

For partners where telco is a suitable brand extension, but not a core business.

Our main contribution

  • Mobile network services
  • Numbering, roaming
  • Operations and business support
  • Product management

Partner's main contribution

  • Marketing, sales and distribution
  • Brand and communication

Light MVNO/
Service Provider

For partners with telco experience who need autonomy in marketing and pricing.

Our main contribution

  • Mobile network services
  • Numbering, roaming
  • Operations and business support

Partner's main contribution

  • Operations and business support
  • Marketing, sales and distribution
  • Brand and communication

Full MVNO/
National Roaming

For partners with significant telco experience and assets.

Our main contribution

  • Mobile (radio) network services
  • Enabling services

Partner's main contribution

  • Core network services
  • Numbering, roaming
  • Operations and business support
  • Marketing, sales and distribution
  • Brand and communication
